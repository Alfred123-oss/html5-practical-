Healthy poultry at Alfred’s farm

Healthy poultry are the foundation of a successful farm. They require balanced nutrition, clean water, spacious housing, and protection from diseases through regular vaccinations and biosecurity measures. Active, well-feathered birds grow faster, produce quality meat and eggs, and ensure sustainable poultry farming that benefits both farmers and consumers alike.

Egg collection at the farm

Egg collection is a vital daily routine in poultry management. It should be done frequently, at least twice a day, to maintain freshness and reduce breakages. Clean, dry hands and sanitized trays help prevent contamination. Prompt collection also minimizes egg damage, ensures higher market value, and improves overall flock productivity.
